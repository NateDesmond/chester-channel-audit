### Your Two Key Stats
Big dashboard:

**CTR (Click-through rate)**
"What % of people who see your thumbnail actually click"
- Under 4% = Thumbnail/title needs work
- 4-7% = Good!
- 7%+ = Crushing it!

**AVD (Average view duration)**
"What % of your video do people watch"
- Under 40% = Losing people early
- 40-50% = Decent

## Design System

### Colors

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | Dark | #0F1419 |
| Surface | Dark grey | #1A1F26 |
| Primary | Forest green | #1B4D3E |
| Secondary | Lake blue | #2E86AB |
| Accent | Golden/amber | #F6AE2D |
| Success | Green | #4CAF50 |
| Text primary | White | #FFFFFF |
| Text secondary | Grey | #A0AEC0 |

**Key decision:** No red anywhere. Use amber/gold for "needs work" - feels like opportunity, not failure.

### Typography
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Numbers/Stats: Space Grotesk

### Components
- Cards with subtle glow on hover
- Gradient borders on key sections
- Glassmorphism panels
- Progress bar visible on all pages
- Star ratings (not number scores)
- Confetti animation at 100% mission completion

### Animations
- Number counters animate on scroll (Intersection Observer)
- Progress bar fills smoothly
- Subtle hover effects on interactive elements
- Page transitions (CSS fade)
- Confetti on completing all missions
