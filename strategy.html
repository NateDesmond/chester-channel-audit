How YouTube Decides What to Show People

It's actually pretty simple:

CTR + AVD = YouTube Shows Your Video

CTR (Click-Through Rate)

What % of people click your thumbnail

If your thumbnail is good, more people click!

AVD (Average View Duration)

How long people watch your video

If your content is good, people stay longer!

Right now you probably have:
Low CTR (thumbnails need work) + Medium AVD (content is good!)

Fix the CTR and your views will grow!
